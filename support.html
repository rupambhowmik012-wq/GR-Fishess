Quick Help

How can I track my order?

Our delivery partner will contact you shortly once the order leaves. Real-time updates will be provided directly to your registered number via SMS and WhatsApp.

What is the return policy?

Returns are only accepted at the time of delivery if the freshness is not satisfactory. Please check your items before the partner leaves.

Is delivery available in my area?

We currently serve all areas within a 15km radius of Binnaguri. You can verify your location by entering your pincode at the checkout page.
